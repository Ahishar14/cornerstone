Make a Difference

Your Gift Builds
A Child's Future

Every contribution — large or small — directly funds scholarships, learning materials, and the infrastructure that allows Cornerstone to serve every child who walks through our gates.

Make a Donation

Secure payment via Flutterwave. Accepts MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, Visa, and Mastercard.

Payments are processed securely by Flutterwave. Cornerstone Schools does not store your card details. You will receive an email receipt once your donation is confirmed.

Your Impact

Every Shilling Has a Name

Donations go directly into the Cornerstone Schools Community Fund, audited annually and administered by our Board of Governors. No gift is wasted.

UGX 50k

Full set of textbooks

Provides one child with all required textbooks for an entire term.

UGX 120k

School uniform + shoes

Kits out a scholarship pupil so they arrive at school with dignity and pride.

UGX 200k

One month's school fees

Covers a scholarship pupil's full monthly fees, allowing them to remain in school.

UGX 500k

Library books

Adds ten new books to the Cornerstone library, expanding what every pupil can read and discover.

UGX 1M+

Full-term sponsorship

Sponsors a child's complete term — fees, meals, uniform, and materials — a transformative gift.

Other Ways to Give

Support Goes Beyond Money

Bank Transfer

Direct bank transfers are welcome. Contact our bursar at finance@cornerstoneschools.ug for our banking details.

In-Kind Donations

We gratefully accept donations of books, stationery, sporting equipment, and computers. Contact us to arrange a drop-off or collection.

Corporate Partnership

Partner with Cornerstone as a corporate sponsor and make a lasting, visible difference to children's education in Kampala. Packages available from UGX 5M.
