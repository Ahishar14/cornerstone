Our Programmes

Rigorous Learning.
Inspired Teaching.

From a child's first day in Baby Class to their final PLE examinations, every stage of a Cornerstone education is deliberately designed for depth, joy, and excellence.

Curriculum Framework

Built on the Uganda National Curriculum

We follow the Uganda National Curriculum as prescribed by the National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC), enriched with additional resources, teaching methodologies, and co-curricular activities drawn from international best practice.

Our class sizes are deliberately kept small — a maximum of 35 pupils per class — so that every child receives genuine attention, timely feedback, and the personalised support they need to excel.

Ages 3–5

Nursery Section

Baby Class · Middle Class · Top Class

Our Nursery section is where the adventure begins. Qualified early-years educators guide children through structured play, story time, number work, and creative activities in a warm, secure environment that feels just like home.

We follow an integrated early-childhood curriculum that develops language, numeracy, fine motor skills, social awareness, and emotional intelligence — laying a rock-solid foundation for primary school.

Phonics & Literacy Number Work Creative Arts Outdoor Play Music & Movement Social Skills

Ages 6–9

Lower Primary

Primary One · Primary Two · Primary Three · Primary Four

The lower primary years are critical for building the academic habits that determine long-term success. Our teachers use active, engaging methodologies to ensure children truly understand — not just memorise — what they are learning.

Reading programmes, mathematics mastery, and regular project-based learning develop independence and curiosity across every subject.

English Language Mathematics Luganda Science & Technology Social Studies Religious Education Creative Arts

Ages 10–13

Upper Primary

Primary Five · Primary Six · Primary Seven

Upper Primary at Cornerstone is where years of careful preparation bear fruit. With disciplined PLE readiness from P5, our pupils approach their UNEB examinations not with fear, but with calm confidence rooted in deep understanding.

Our P7 results consistently place us among the highest-performing schools in Kampala, with graduates regularly gaining entry to the most sought-after secondary schools in Uganda.

English Mathematics Science Social Studies Luganda / Local Language Religious Education

Beyond the Classroom

A Child is More Than Their Grades

Our co-curricular programme is as carefully designed as our academics — because sport, art, drama, and service develop the whole person.

Sport

Football, netball, athletics, swimming, and volleyball. Inter-school competitions build sportsmanship and teamwork alongside physical fitness.

Arts & Culture

School choir, drama club, traditional dance, and visual arts. Annual Cultural Day celebrates Uganda's rich heritage through performance and creativity.

Leadership & Service

Prefect programme, peer mentorship, and community service projects. Pupils learn early that leadership is service — and that they have the power to make a difference.
