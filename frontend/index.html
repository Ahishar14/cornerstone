Our Story

A Foundation Built on Character and Scholarship

Cornerstone Schools was founded with a singular conviction: that every child in Uganda deserves a world-class education grounded in values, discipline, and curiosity. From nursery through Primary Seven, we provide a nurturing environment where young minds flourish.

Our approach blends the best of the Ugandan national curriculum with international best practices, producing pupils who are confident, articulate, and prepared for the challenges ahead.

What We Stand For

Three Pillars of a Cornerstone Education

01

Academic Rigour

Our curriculum challenges every pupil to think critically, ask bold questions, and master subjects from literacy and numeracy to science, social studies, and the arts — all within the Uganda National Curriculum framework.

02

Character Formation

We believe education is incomplete without moral grounding. Integrity, responsibility, empathy, and service to community are woven into every day at Cornerstone — from morning assembly to the final bell.

03

Holistic Growth

Sport, music, drama, and co-curricular activities are not extras — they are essential. A child who discovers their passion for football or the choir develops confidence that transforms their academic journey too.
