Our Story

More Than a School —
A Community of Purpose

Since 2010, Cornerstone has been the school of choice for families in Budaka who believe that education should challenge, inspire, and transform.

Mission & Vision

Shaping Young Minds for a Changing World

Our Mission

To provide every child with an academically excellent, morally grounded, and holistically enriching education that prepares them for a life of purpose, leadership, and positive contribution to Uganda and the world.

Our Vision

To be Uganda's most respected nursery and primary school — a community where rigorous learning, genuine care, and deep values converge to produce the next generation of principled leaders.

Our Motto

"Build on a Sure Foundation"

What We Believe

The Values That Define Us

01

Integrity

We teach children that how you achieve something matters as much as whether you achieve it. Honesty, fairness, and moral courage are practised — not merely preached — in every classroom.

02

Excellence

We hold our pupils to high standards because we believe in their capacity. Excellence is not perfection — it is sustained effort, growth, and the relentless pursuit of one's personal best.

03

Compassion

A Cornerstone child is taught to see the humanity in others — to be a thoughtful classmate, a responsible community member, and ultimately a caring citizen of Uganda and the world.

04

Perseverance

Challenges are not obstacles — they are part of the journey. We cultivate resilience by celebrating effort, encouraging persistence, and modelling the grit our teachers themselves embody.

05

Community

School is not just a building — it is a community. Parents, teachers, pupils, and staff are all partners in education. Cornerstone's warmth and belonging is felt by every family from day one.
